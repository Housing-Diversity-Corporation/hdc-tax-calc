# Investor & HDC Net Benefits Summary

## Example Project: $100M Opportunity Zone Development
**95% Leveraged Structure with Philanthropic Debt**

---

## Project Parameters
- **Total Project Cost**: $100 million
- **Land Value**: $10 million (10% of project)
- **Building/Improvements**: $90 million
- **Year 1 NOI**: $5.113 million (5.113% cap rate)
- **Year 1 Depreciation**: 25% of building value
- **Location**: New York (10.9% state tax rate)
- **OZ Type**: Standard (10% basis step-up)

## Capital Structure (95% Leverage)
| Source | Amount | % of Total | Rate | Terms |
|--------|--------|------------|------|--------|
| **Investor Equity** | $5M | 5% | - | 100% from deferred capital gains |
| **Senior Debt** | $66M | 66% | 5% | 35-year amortization |
| **Philanthropic Debt** | $20M | 20% | 0% | 60-year amortization (Ballmer Group) |
| **HDC Sub Debt** | $2M | 2% | 8% PIK | 50% current pay |
| **Investor Sub Debt** | $2.5M | 2.5% | 8% PIK | 50% current pay |
| **Outside Investor Sub Debt** | $4.5M | 4.5% | 8% PIK | 50% current pay |
| **Total** | **$100M** | **100%** | | |

## INVESTOR NET BENEFITS

### 1. **Immediate Tax Benefits (Year 1)**
- **Gross Depreciation Benefit**: $22.5M × 47.9% = **$10.78M**
  - Federal: 37% × $22.5M = $8.33M
  - NY State: 10.9% × $22.5M = $2.45M
- **HDC Fee**: $10.78M × 10% = ($1.08M)
- **Net Year 1 Tax Benefit**: **$9.70M**
- **Coverage of $5M equity**: **194%** - Nearly 2X return in Year 1!

### 2. **OZ Capital Gains Tax Deferral & Reduction**
- **Deferred Gains**: $5M (investor's original gains)
- **Tax Deferral**: Defer ~$1.73M in taxes (34.65% rate) until Year 5
- **Basis Step-Up**: 10% × $5M = $500k permanently forgiven
- **Tax Savings from Step-Up**: $500k × 34.65% = **$173k permanent savings**
- **Year 5 Tax Due**: $4.5M × 34.65% = $1.56M
  - *Note: Year 5 depreciation of ~$1.8M (tax value ~$860k) helps offset this payment*
  - **Net Year 5 Impact**: $1.56M tax - $0.86M benefit = ~$700k net payment

### 3. **Cash Flow During Hold (Years 1-10)**
- **Annual Operating Cash Flow**: ~$500k-$1M/year
- **Interest from Sub Debt**: $200k/year (8% on $2.5M)
- **Total 10-Year Operating Returns**: ~$7-10M

### 4. **Exit Value (Year 10)**
- **Property Value** (6% exit cap): ~$110M
- **Less Debt Payoff**: ~($85M)
- **Investor Share of Equity** (65% after promote): ~**$16.25M**
- **Plus Accumulated PIK**: ~$3.5M
- **Total Exit Proceeds**: ~**$20M**

### 5. **OZ Capital Gains Exclusion at Exit**
- **Appreciation on $5M investment**: ~$15M gain
- **Tax Savings** (0% tax after 10 years): $15M × 34.65% = **$5.2M saved**

## KEY INSIGHTS

### Why Year 5 OZ Tax is a Net Benefit
1. **Tax is on reduced basis**: Only pay tax on 90% of gains (10% step-up)
2. **Depreciation offset**: Year 5 still has ~$1.8M depreciation ($860k tax value)
3. **Time value of money**: 5-year deferral worth ~25-30% in present value
4. **Net cost is minimal**: ~$700k net in Year 5 vs. $9.7M Year 1 benefit

### The "Free Investment" Timeline
- **Year 1**: Recover 194% of equity through tax benefits
- **Years 2-3**: Additional depreciation covers remaining basis
- **Years 4-10**: Pure profit accumulation
- **Exit**: Tax-free appreciation on entire investment

### Risk Mitigation
- **95% leverage** minimizes investor capital at risk
- **Philanthropic debt** at 0% reduces debt service burden
- **Tax benefits** provide immediate return of capital
- **OZ structure** ensures tax-efficient exit

### Why This Works
1. **Depreciation Arbitrage**: Convert 47.9% tax rate into 10% HDC fee = 79% efficiency
2. **Leverage Amplification**: 95% leverage means tax benefits exceed equity investment
3. **Philanthropic Support**: 0% debt dramatically improves cash flow
4. **OZ Tax Benefits**: Triple benefit (deferral + step-up + exclusion)
5. **Time Value**: Immediate benefits vs. deferred liabilities

## CONCLUSION

The HDC Opportunity Zone structure with 95% leverage and philanthropic debt creates a remarkable win-win:

- **Investors**: Turn $5M into ~$36-39M (720-780% ROI) with immediate capital recovery
- **HDC**: Generate $4.68M+ from $2M investment with minimal risk
- **Community**: $100M of new development with affordable capital structure
- **Tax Efficiency**: ~$16M in total tax benefits properly utilized

The Year 5 OZ tax payment ($1.56M) is insignificant compared to the total benefits and is partially offset by ongoing depreciation. This structure essentially provides investors with a "free" $100M property after tax benefits, making it one of the most compelling investment structures available in real estate today.
